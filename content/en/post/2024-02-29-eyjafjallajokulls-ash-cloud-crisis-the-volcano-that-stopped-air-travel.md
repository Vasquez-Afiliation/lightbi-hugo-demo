## Aviation Safety Concerns

Aviation industry experts recognize volcanic ash as a critical safety concern for aircraft engines due to its abrasive nature and potential to cause significant damage. Volcanic ash particles, when ingested by jet engines, can lead to engine failure by clogging fuel nozzles and eroding turbine blades. This poses a serious risk to air travel safety, as it can result in reduced thrust and even potential flameouts in flight.
